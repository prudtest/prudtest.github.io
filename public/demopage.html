Inventory Performance vs. Sales

Description:

This report presents an analysis of the performance of the inventory to meet the sales demand. The report presents two types of graphs. A graph representing the demand and available quantities side by side and another graph to demonstrate the difference between the inventory and sales demand. In both cases, the sales forecast quantity can be added to the sales quantity as an option
